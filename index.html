Pine Kazi is a new and innovative social business venture that enables a circular economy by using pineapple waste produced after harvest to craft eco-friendly products like shoes and bags. Our cradle-to-cradle approach encourages pineapple farming communities to move from a linear model of plant-harvest-dispose to a circular model of plant-harvest-reuse.

Our Journey ðŸš€

Pine Kazi company limited was started by three passionate student enterpreneurs in 2019 who realised that crop waste management is a serious issue in Kenya which often goes ignored and uncontested. Our main inspiration came about when we visited Delmonte Kenya(the largest pineapple producing company in Kenya) and realised that the after-harvest waste is usually burnt or left in the farm to decompose, releasing tonnes of harmful green-house gases into the environment which compelled us to start.
